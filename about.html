About Living Waters

Founded in 1985, Living Waters Senior Secondary has been at the forefront of secondary education, providing a nurturing environment where students can achieve their full potential.

Our History

Living Waters Senior Secondary was established in 1985 with a vision to create an educational institution that would not only excel academically but also foster character development and holistic growth.

Over the past three decades, we have grown from a small community school to a leading senior secondary institution, consistently achieving outstanding academic results while maintaining our commitment to our founding principles.

Our alumni network spans across the globe, with graduates excelling in various fields including medicine, engineering, arts, business, and public service.

Our Vision & Mission

Our Vision

"At Living Waters Senior Secondary, we envision a learning community where every student discovers their unique potential and develops the knowledge, skills, and character to thrive in an ever-changing world."

"We aspire to be a beacon of educational excellence, fostering innovation, critical thinking, and global citizenship while maintaining our commitment to traditional values of integrity, respect, and responsibility."

Our Mission

"Our mission is to provide a comprehensive and challenging educational experience that empowers students to become lifelong learners, ethical leaders, and compassionate contributors to society."

"We are dedicated to cultivating intellectual curiosity, fostering creativity, and developing well-rounded individuals through a balanced curriculum that integrates academics, arts, athletics, and community service."

"Living Waters Senior Secondary strives to create a supportive and inclusive environment where students from diverse backgrounds can achieve academic excellence while developing strong moral character and social consciousness."

Our Values

Excellence

We strive for the highest standards in all aspects of education, encouraging students to reach their full potential.

Integrity

We foster honesty, ethical behavior, and moral courage in our students and staff.

Respect

We value diversity and treat all individuals with dignity, compassion, and understanding.

Community

We build strong relationships and work collaboratively for the betterment of our school and society.
