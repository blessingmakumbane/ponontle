About Magalanngwe Senior Secondary

Founded in 1987, Magalanngwe Senior Secondary Senior Secondary has been at the forefront of secondary education, providing a nurturing environment where students can achieve their full potential.

Our History

Magalanngwe Senior Secondary Senior Secondary was established in 1987 with a vision to create an educational institution that would not only excel academically but also foster character development and holistic growth.

Over the past three decades, we have grown from a small community school to a leading senior secondary institution, consistently achieving outstanding academic results while maintaining our commitment to our founding principles.

Our Vision & Mission

Our Vision

"To provide quality education to learners in order to uplift and empower them to play a responsible and meaningful role in the society"

"We aspire to be a beacon of educational excellence, fostering innovation,"

Our Mission

"Our mission is to provide a comprehensive and challenging educational experience that empowers students to become lifelong learners, ethical leaders, and compassionate contributors to society."

"We are dedicated to cultivating intellectual curiosity, fostering creativity, and developing well-rounded individuals through a balanced curriculum"

"Magalanngwe Senior Secondary strives to encourage parental and community involvement in the school, to provide quality education through instruction, guidance, and facilitation with care, dedication, and accountability."

Our Values

Excellence

We strive for the highest standards in all aspects of education, encouraging students to reach their full potential.

Integrity

We foster honesty, ethical behavior, and moral courage in our students and staff.

Respect

We value diversity and treat all individuals with dignity, compassion, and understanding.

Community

We build strong relationships and work collaboratively for the betterment of our school and society.
