Academic Programs

At Living Waters Senior Secondary, we offer a comprehensive and challenging curriculum designed to prepare students for success in university and beyond.

Our Academic Programs

Sciences Program

Our comprehensive sciences curriculum prepares students for university studies in medicine, engineering, and research fields through hands-on laboratory work and advanced theoretical concepts.

Program Highlights:

  • Advanced Placement courses
  • State-of-the-art laboratories
  • Science fair participation
  • Research mentorship program
  • University partnerships

Humanities & Arts

Our humanities program develops critical thinking, communication skills, and cultural awareness through the study of literature, history, philosophy, and the arts.

Program Highlights:

  • Advanced literature courses
  • Debate and public speaking
  • Creative writing workshops
  • Visual and performing arts
  • Cultural exchange programs

Mathematics & Technology

We offer a rigorous mathematics curriculum complemented by cutting-edge technology education, preparing students for careers in STEM fields.

Program Highlights:

  • Advanced mathematics courses
  • Computer programming
  • Robotics and engineering
  • Data science introduction
  • Technology competitions

Athletics Program

Our comprehensive athletics program promotes physical fitness, teamwork, and sportsmanship while offering competitive opportunities in various sports.

Program Highlights:

  • Varsity sports teams
  • Fitness and wellness education
  • Intramural competitions
  • Sports scholarship guidance
  • Leadership development

College Preparation

We provide comprehensive college counseling and preparation services to ensure our students are competitive applicants to top universities worldwide.

Program Highlights:

  • Personalized counseling
  • SAT/ACT preparation
  • Application essay workshops
  • University visit programs
  • Scholarship assistance

Language Programs

Our language programs develop proficiency in multiple languages, fostering global citizenship and cross-cultural understanding.

Program Highlights:

  • English language mastery
  • French and Spanish options
  • Cultural immersion activities
  • Language certification
  • Exchange programs

Curriculum Overview

Our curriculum is designed to meet international standards while addressing local educational requirements. We follow a balanced approach that emphasizes:

Core Subjects

Mathematics, Sciences, Languages, Social Studies, and Information Technology form the foundation of our academic program.

Elective Courses

Students can choose from a wide range of electives including Advanced Sciences, Business Studies, Visual Arts, Music, and Computer Programming.
