Admissions Process

Step 1: Inquiry

Begin by submitting an inquiry form or contacting our admissions office. We'll provide detailed information about our programs and answer any questions you may have.

Step 2: School Tour

Schedule a visit to experience our facilities, meet our faculty, and see our learning environment firsthand.

Step 3: Application

Complete the application form and submit all required documents, including academic records and recommendation letters.

Step 4: Assessment

Applicants will be invited for an assessment and interview to determine their readiness for our academic program.

Step 5: Decision

Our admissions committee reviews each application carefully and notifies families of decisions within two weeks of assessment.

Step 6: Enrollment

Accepted students complete enrollment forms and submit required deposits to secure their place at Magalanngwe.

Admission Requirements

Academic Requirements

Completed application form with recent photograph

Official Reports from previous academic year & Current year if Applicable

Teacher's recommendations

Additional Documents

ID/ Birth certificate or passport copy

Personal statement from student
