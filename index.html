About Living Waters

Founded in 1985, Living Waters Senior Secondary has been at the forefront of secondary education, providing a nurturing environment where students can achieve their full potential.

Our school combines academic rigor with a strong emphasis on character development, ensuring our graduates are not only prepared for higher education but also equipped to become responsible, contributing members of society.
